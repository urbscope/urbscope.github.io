About

The tourism industry is one of the largest industries in the world with global nancial contributions of 7.6 trillion dollars in 2015 alone. However, only a handful of apps exist to facilitate tourists in their travels, and this poses great issues for the industry. Hence Urbscope is developed to address the issues that subsist in the industry, to facilitate global tourism and enhance the everlasting experiences of dauntless travelers.

Your pilot

The app makes it easier for you to locate points of interests via interactive augmented reality markers displayed on your screen as you scan your horizon using your phone's camera. Upon selecting the point of interest you want to visit by clicking on the marker, you can then navigate to the location.

Your tour guide

The app allows you to obtain exciting information pertaining to touristic landmarks. You can use Urbscope to identify the landmark and accordingly get insightful information of that landmark.

Your event coordinator

The app provides personalized recommendations of points of interest based upon your likes and dislikes. There are a thousand and one things you can do, but Urbscope shall recommend those things to you that you would personally love to do.
