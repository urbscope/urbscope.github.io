What is Urbscope?

Most of tourists know about neither the details of historical landmarks nor which places to visit in a city. Looking up this information before or during the tour can be time-consuming and prone to be forgotten. Therefore, Urbscope is designed to solve all the issues that modern travellers face when they are on a thrilling adventure. It is a mobile application that will make sightseeing more meaningful by facilitating information acquisition about tourist attractions. Urbscope is able to detect surrounding landmarks and provide all you need to know about them. It also offers personalized landmark recommendations and navigation. By putting all the touristic information into the palms of the user, Urbscope is aimed at revolutionising the tourism and travel industry.

Detect Landmarks

Urbscope will detect and attempt to recognise landmarks through your phone's camera. After detecting a landmark it will inform you about the landmark's history and details.

Explore Nearby Locations

Explore landmarks in your vicinity filtered by the categories you are interested in, and then find your way to the destination you chose through Urbscope's 3D navigation assistant

Get Personalised Recommendations

Rate the sites you have visited in order to get personalised recommendations for places that might interest you
